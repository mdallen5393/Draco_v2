Our Story

DraCo is the ultimate destination for discerning dragon owners who want to pamper their scaly friends. We specialize in high-end products and services that enhance the beauty, comfort, and performance of your dragons, from body care and luxury sleep items to riding apparel and accessories. Our staff are experts in dragon fashion and wellness who can help you find the perfect fit for your dragon’s personality and needs. We are the only boutique in world that has been awarded a seal by The Ministry of Magic. DraCo is not just a boutique, it’s a lifestyle for those who love dragons and appreciate their elegance and grace.
